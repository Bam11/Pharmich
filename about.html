The Pharmich Journey

Our founders established Pharmich in 2013

Founded in 2013, Pharmich emerged from a vision to transform industrial cleaning from a necessary expense into a strategic investment for operational excellence. Our founder, with extensive experience in industrial safety and compliance, recognized a critical gap in the market: manufacturing and healthcare facilities were struggling to find cleaning partners who truly understood their specialized environments.

What began as a specialized service for pharmaceutical manufacturing facilities has evolved into a comprehensive industrial cleaning and facility management company serving multiple sectors where cleanliness directly impacts operational performance, regulatory compliance, and safety outcomes.

Today, Pharmich serves over 500 clients across manufacturing, pharmaceutical, healthcare, food processing, and commercial sectors, delivering specialized cleaning and facility management solutions that exceed industry standards. Our growth has been built on a foundation of technical expertise, rigorous protocols, and unwavering commitment to our clients' operational success.

The Pharmich Methodology

Our approach to industrial cleaning and facility management goes beyond standard cleaning practices, implementing a systematic methodology that ensures consistent, verifiable results.

Comprehensive Assessment

Before implementing any cleaning program, we conduct a thorough evaluation of your facility, analyzing operational workflows, identifying critical areas, and understanding specific contamination challenges.

Custom Protocol Development

Based on our assessment, we develop facility-specific cleaning protocols that address your unique requirements, compliance needs, and operational constraints.

Resource Allocation

We assign specialized equipment and technicians with relevant expertise to your facility, ensuring that you have the right resources for optimal cleaning outcomes.

Implementation with Precision

Our teams execute cleaning protocols with meticulous attention to detail, following established procedures while minimizing disruption to your operations.

Documentation & Verification

Continuous Improvement

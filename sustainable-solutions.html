Electronics Manufacturer Sustainability Transformation

Electronics Manufacturing

45% Water Reduction
60% Chemical Reduction
75% Waste Reduction

The Challenge

An electronics manufacturer seeking to improve their environmental profile while maintaining the strict cleanliness requirements of their production environment.

Our Solution

Pharmich implemented a comprehensive sustainable cleaning program that integrated water conservation, chemical reduction, and waste minimization while maintaining the strict cleanliness requirements of their production environment.

  • Water-efficient cleaning equipment with reclamation systems
  • Transition to concentrated, green-certified cleaning products
  • Implementation of microfiber technology with on-site laundering
  • Staff training on sustainable cleaning practices
  • Environmental monitoring and performance tracking system

The Results

Within one year, the program achieved a 45% reduction in water consumption, 60% decrease in chemical usage, and 75% reduction in cleaning-related waste, contributing significantly to the facility's overall sustainability metrics. The client also reported improved workplace satisfaction and enhanced positioning with environmentally-conscious customers.

"Pharmich's sustainable solutions program transformed our approach to facility cleaning. Not only did they maintain the strict cleanliness standards our electronics production requires, but they also delivered remarkable environmental improvements that have become a highlight of our corporate sustainability reporting."

Sustainability Director, Electronics Manufacturer

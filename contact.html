Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us and our service process.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For standard service requests, we typically schedule an initial consultation within 2-3 business days. For urgent needs, we can often accommodate same-day or next-day meetings. Our emergency response team is available 24/7 for critical situations requiring immediate attention.

Do you provide services nationwide?

Yes, Pharmich serves industrial facilities across the United States through our regional offices and service centers. We maintain consistent service quality and standards across all locations to ensure reliability regardless of your facility's location.

How are your services priced?

Our services are priced based on facility size, specific requirements, frequency of service, and customized program elements. After an initial consultation and/or facility assessment, we provide detailed proposals with transparent pricing for your specific needs.

Do you offer one-time services or only contracts?

We offer both one-time specialized cleaning services and ongoing maintenance contracts. Many clients begin with a one-time service to address specific issues, then transition to regular maintenance programs after experiencing our results. Our flexible service models are designed to accommodate your specific needs.

Are your cleaning products and methods safe for sensitive environments?

Absolutely. We select appropriate cleaning products and methodologies based on your specific environment and requirements. For sensitive areas like food processing, pharmaceutical production, or electronics manufacturing, we implement specialized protocols designed for those environments with products that are effective yet safe for critical applications.

What information should I prepare before requesting a quote?

To help us provide the most accurate quote, it's helpful to have information about your facility size, industry, specific cleaning challenges, service frequency needs, and any regulatory requirements. However, if you don't have all this information readily available, our team can work with you during the consultation to determine your needs and provide appropriate recommendations.
